Slow Cooker Mexican Quinoa

Description

Throw all of the ingredients for this Healthy Slow Cooker NC Sweetpotato Mexican Quinoa into your slow cooker for an easy weeknight dinner thatâ€™s plant-based and made with pantry staples!

This vegan crock pot recipe is such a delicious and wholesome stay-at-home meal that will last you all week long!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup uncooked white quinoa
  • 2 large NC sweetpotatoes, peeled and diced
  • 2 large NC sweetpotatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 15-oz. can whole kernel corn, drained and rinsed
  • 1 28-oz. can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 4-oz. can green chiles
  • 2 cups vegetable broth (or any kind of broth)
  • 1/2 large red onion, finely diced
  • 3 tablespoons taco seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon sriracha
  • 2 teaspoons maple syrup
Optional Toppings:
  • fresh cilantro
  • yogurt, dairy-free if necessary
  • sliced avocado
  • smoked paprika

Steps

  1. Begin by spraying your slow cooker with non-stick cooking spray.
  2. Then, place all of the ingredients into your slow cooker and mix until combined.
  3. Cover and cook on high for 3-4 hours or on low for 6-8 hours (or until the quinoa is fully cooked and the sweet potatoes are at desired texture).
  4. Occasionally stir all of the ingredients together every hour.
  5. When the quinoa is cooked and the sweet potatoes are soft, uncover the slow cooker.
  6. Serve the sweetpotato Mexican quinoa with the optional toppings and enjoy!
